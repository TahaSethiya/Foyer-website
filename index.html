Foyer Bench

About

The Foyer Bench is a simple and elegant piece of furniture that is perfect for any entryway. It is made from solid wood and features a comfortable cushioned seat. The bench is designed to provide a place to sit while putting on or taking off shoes, and also offers storage space underneath the seat. The Foyer Bench is available in a variety of finishes to complement any decor.
